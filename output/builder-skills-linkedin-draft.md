## The Post

Every AI app builder gives you the same AI. Same prompts, same behavior, same limitations — no matter what you're building.

That never made sense to us.

A builder making an internal HR tool doesn't need the same AI behavior as someone building a customer-facing marketplace. The prompts are different, the tone is different, the guardrails are different. But every platform treats builders like they all want the same thing.

We're shipping Builder Skills next week. Workspace-level customization for how your AI builder actually works.

Add a skill, toggle it per app, turn it off when you don't need it. Your builder, your rules.

The idea is simple: skills are instructions that shape how the builder behaves in your context. Want it to follow your brand guidelines? That's a skill. Want it to always use a specific API pattern? Skill. Want it to stop suggesting approaches you've already rejected? Also a skill.

It's not a settings page. It's giving builders real control over the AI they rely on every day.

Shipping Feb 25.
