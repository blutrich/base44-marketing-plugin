Frequently
asked questions

What is the Base44 Backend?

The Base44 Backend is an AI-first backend that lets teams build, connect, and run production-grade applications without managing infrastructure. It handles data, logic, integrations, and permissions behind the scenes, so you can focus on solving real problems and shipping more products.

How is the Base44 Backend different from other BaaS platforms?

Unlike traditional BaaS platforms that require manual configuration, Base44 is built for AI-native workflows. Everything is code-first and CLI-driven, meaning AI agents can define your data model, set permissions, and deploy your backend without ever leaving the terminal.

What does it mean to be an AI-first backend?

AI-first means the entire platform is designed around how AI agents work. The CLI provides structured, predictable interfaces that agents can use directly. Skills reduce rework by giving agents pre-built patterns. And the declarative data model lets agents define backends in TypeScript without needing to understand infrastructure details.

Is Base44 production-ready?

Yes. Base44 is the trusted backend for over 10 million apps. It includes CDN-backed hosting, automatic HTTPS, row-level security, and one-command deployment. Teams are shipping production applications with real users on the platform every day.
