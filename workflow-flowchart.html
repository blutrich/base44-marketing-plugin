Workflow Overview

  • 8 Content Types: PAID_AD, LINKEDIN, X, EMAIL, LANDING, SEO, VIDEO, CAMPAIGN

  • Quality Gate: Brand Guardian (Haiku) ensures brand compliance
  • Feedback Loop: Non-compliant content is refined until approved
