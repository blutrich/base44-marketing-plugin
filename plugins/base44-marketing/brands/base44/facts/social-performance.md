## What Works (Pattern Tracking)

| Content Type | Performance | Notes |
|--------------|-------------|-------|
| Milestone posts ($100M ARR) | Viral | Maor personal + brand account amplification |
| Feature teasers (Agents) | High demand | 700+ early access requests |
| Cultural moment content (Super Bowl) | Viral | 47% reach, 62% reposts from one event |
| Connector announcements | Solid | Consistent engagement |
| Skills/upgrade posts | Strong | Deep comments on LinkedIn |
| Ambassadors Program | Strong | Cross-platform engagement |
| User spotlight / success stories | Strong | Builds community proof |

## What Doesn't Work

| Content Type | Performance | Notes |
|--------------|-------------|-------|
| Talking-to-camera video | Underperforms | Static/carousel beats video on LinkedIn |
| Sunday posting | Lower reach | Carousel on Sunday had reduced engagement |
| High-volume posting | Diminishing returns | 5 posts/week with highest engagement per post |
