# Base44 CTAs Library

> Call-to-action templates by channel and context. Always action-oriented.

---

## Primary CTAs

### Direct Action
- "Start building"
- "Try Base44 free"
- "Build your first app"
- "Ship something today"

### Soft Invite
- "See what's possible"
- "Watch builders ship"
- "Explore the platform"

---

## Channel-Specific CTAs

### LinkedIn
- "Happy building!"
- "We can't wait to see what you build."
- "Drop your app idea below."
- "Link in comments." (never in main post)

### X (Twitter)
- "Try it: base44.com"
- "Build something wild."
- Thread endings: "That's the playbook. Now go ship."

### Discord
- "Happy testing!"
- "Let us know what you think!"
- "Share what you're building."

### Email
- "Start building now"
- "Claim your free credits"
- "See the demo"

---

## Context-Specific CTAs

### Feature Announcements
- "Try it now."
- "Available today."
- "Go test it."

### Case Studies
- "Read the full story."
- "See how they did it."
- "Get the playbook."

### Milestones
- "Join 400K builders."
- "Be part of what's next."

### Tutorials
- "Follow along."
- "Build yours."
- "Try this template."

---

## CTA Anti-Patterns (Never Use)

- Question CTAs: "What would you build?" (feels needy)
- Vague asks: "Learn more" (no action)
- Pressure: "Don't miss out!" (FOMO)
- Corporate: "Contact our sales team"
- Passive: "Feel free to..."

---

## Sign-Off Phrases

### Casual (Discord, X)
- "Happy building!"
- "Go ship something."
- "That's it."

### Supportive (LinkedIn)
- "We can't wait to see what you build."
- "Excited to see what comes next."

### Direct (Email)
- "Let's build."
- "Start now."

---

*Match CTA energy to channel. LinkedIn = supportive. X = punchy. Discord = casual.*
