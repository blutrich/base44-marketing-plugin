## Team Structure

| Brand guardian | 1 | Haiku | output/reviews/ |

## Task Sizing
- 3-5 content pieces per teammate (sweet spot)
- Each piece = 1 task in the shared task list
- Guardian reviews after each piece completes (use task dependencies)
