# Email Blurb Template

## Format Rules
- **Length:** 50-150 words for newsletter blurb
- **Subject line:** Clear value, not clickbait
- **Structure:** Scannable with clear CTA

## Newsletter Blurb Structure

```
**[FEATURE NAME]**

[1 sentence: what it is]

[1-2 sentences: why it matters / problem it solves]

[CTA button or link]
```

## Dedicated Email Structure (Tier 1)

```
Subject: [Clear value proposition]

[Opening hook - 1 sentence]

[What's new - 2-3 sentences]

[Key benefits - bullet points]
• Benefit 1
• Benefit 2
• Benefit 3

[CTA button]

[Sign-off]
```

## Voice Adjustments for Email
- Warm and direct
- Respect their inbox — be concise
- Value-focused, not hype
- Personal but professional
