## Who We Are

**Base44** empowers individuals to actively participate in the coding world. We are enablers — we give people the tools to get the best out of their skills and ideas.

**Mission:** Earn trust and establish Base44 as the home for every builder — from those taking their first steps in vibe coding to major players running their businesses on our platform.
