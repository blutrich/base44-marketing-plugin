# Product Hunt Dominance Playbook

> "Product Hunt isn't won on launch day - it's won in the 8 weeks before."

## The Reality

### What Most Get Wrong
1. Thinking launch day matters (80% determined before)
2. Focusing on product, not people
3. Asking for upvotes cold (spam)

### What Actually Wins
- **Relationship density:** How many PHunters know you?
- **Reciprocity debt:** How many people "owe" you?
- **Community standing:** Are you known for giving?

---

## The 8-Week Protocol

| Week | Focus | Daily Time |
|------|-------|------------|
| 1-2 | Research & List Building | 15 min |
| 3-4 | Initial Outreach & Engagement | 20 min |
| 5-6 | Deepening Relationships | 15 min |
| 7 | Pre-Launch Activation | 30 min |
| 8 | Launch Week | 60+ min |

---

## Weeks 1-2: Research

### Goal: 200+ active PH community members

**Build target list with:**
- Name, PH Username
- Twitter/X, LinkedIn
- What they've launched/hunted
- Notes

**Target Categories:**
| Category | Why | # to Find |
|----------|-----|-----------|
| Prolific Hunters | Can hunt your product | 20 |
| Successful Makers | Reciprocate | 50 |
| Active Commenters | Engaged | 100 |
| Rising Stars | Motivated | 30+ |

---

## Weeks 3-4: Initial Outreach

### Daily 20-Minute Routine

**Minutes 0-10: PH Activity**
- Upvote 3-5 products genuinely
- Leave 2-3 thoughtful comments
- Follow 5 interesting makers

**Minutes 10-20: Maker Outreach**
- Find someone who launched recently
- Use/review their product
- Send DM with specific feedback

### Initial DM Template
```
Hey [Name]!

Just saw [Product] - [specific thing you liked].

Quick question: [genuine question about launch]

I'm building [one sentence], so learning from
how others approach this.

Congrats!
```

---

## Weeks 5-6: Deepening Relationships

### The Reciprocity Trap
Help makers when most vulnerable (during launch). They'll return favor.

**When Someone Launches:**
- Be first to comment (substantive)
- Share on all social channels
- Reply to others' comments
- Send DM with support offer

**Post-Launch DM:**
```
Hey [Name]!

Congrats on [Product] launch!

Anything I can do to help amplify?
- Share to my [X] followers
- Post in [relevant community]
- Write up a quick review

Just let me know!
```

### Relationship Levels
| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| 0 | Stranger |
| 1 | They replied once |
| 2 | Multiple exchanges |
| 3 | Would help each other |
| 4 | Actively promotes you |

**Goal:** 30+ people at Level 2-3 before launch

---

## Week 7: Pre-Launch Activation

**Day 1-2:** Finalize assets (landing page, copy, images)

**Day 3-4:** Soft announcements on social
```
Been working on something for [X months].
Launching on Product Hunt [date].
More soon...
```

**Day 5:** DM Tier A contacts (strongest 20)
```
Launching [Product] on PH [date].
Would love your support. I'll send link morning of!
```

**Day 6:** DM Tier B contacts (next 30-50)

**Day 7:** Final prep, schedule posts

### Best Timing
- **Days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Avoid:** Weekends, Monday, Friday
- **Time:** 12:01 AM PST (maximize 24hr window)

---

## Week 8: Launch Week

### Launch Day Schedule

| Time (PST) | Action |
|------------|--------|
| 12:01 AM | Product live, email blast |
| 6:00 AM | Social posts, DM Tier A |
| 9:00 AM | Monitor comments, DM Tier B |
| 12:00 PM | Second social push |
| 3:00 PM | Thank supporters publicly |
| 6:00 PM | Final social push |
| 11:00 PM | Last push, thank-you prep |

### Reply to EVERY Comment
```
Thanks so much, [Name]!

[Answer their question]

[Add interesting context]

Would love to hear if you try it - let me know!
```

---

## Post-Launch

### Day After
1. Thank EVERYONE who supported
2. Share results publicly
3. Note who came through (for future)
4. Be ready to return favor
